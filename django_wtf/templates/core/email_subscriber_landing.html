Keep up with Django OSS

I built this site to discover new Django projects in the open source world. I previously relied on Github newsletters, but Github notified me of projects that had nothing to do with Django.

Django.WTF sends emails every other week that contain trending and popular repositories from the Django world. The project also scrapes Hacker News and Reddit for upvoted posts.

Every two weeks an email is sent out with:

  • Top trending projects by growth %
  • Trending social media news
  • The engineers that maintain the most popular projects

I use it to keep up on what's happening and so could you.
